Heavenly Father,
As members of the Bosco house family,
we strive to follow in the pathway of our saint, John Bosco to follow his teachings of love and faith, and to see us all as unique individuals, that are created equally.
Give us the courage to follow the example set by St John Bosco, to be holy, compassionate and to love God.
To accept all responsibilities and show kindness, to be a friend to all and inspire others.
We are blessed to be in this community, we pray that we model Salesian values, and be responsible, respectful, honest learners.
As we play, learn and love one another.

St John Bosco
Pray for us.

Heavenly Father,
We gather as members of the Guzman House, to celebrate and remember our patron saint, Dominic Guzman.
In this house we strive to always follow in his footsteps, and to follow your teachings of knowledge and compassion.
As part of Guzman house we gather here as five different pastoral groups, as one whole collective, as one family. May we share your guidance and your teachings and use these all throughout our lives, and know that we are all created and treated equally.
We are blessed to be in this community together in the company of each other. With your guidance we can follow the virtues of Dominic College, of being responsible, respectful, honest learners.
As we play, learn and love one another.

St Dominic Guzman
Pray for us.

Heavenly Father,
We are a blessed family, a Savio family, we hope to bring laughter and joy into the lives of others, to bring happiness to all we meet.
Help us to bring friendship and understanding to everyone, to be someone who can listen to others and understand.
We thank you for opportunity to connect with others, and thank you for the joy it bring us, as we work towards making the world in your image, and keeping it a sacred place for all.
Help us take the teachings of St Dominic Savio into our hearts, as we learn to be understanding and kind to our peers, may you encourage us to look after others and always be loyal to you.

St Dominic Savio,
Pray for us.

Heavenly Father,
We are gathered here as one Siena family, to spread love and wisdom among our house and our peers.
We ask the Holy Spirit to give us courage and strength as we walk the journey of life.
We pray for healing and comfort to be received by those who need it most, and for all to have joy and peace.
Help us to lead and support all those in our College community, let us aspire to be wise and courageous like St Catherine of Siena, to be charitable and kind-hearted.
May we bring peace to all we encounter and lead by example to make the world a better place.

St Catherine of Siena,
Pray for us.

History of our Saints

BOSCO: ls named after St John Bosco (1815-1888), the friend of youth. He lived in Turin, Italy and established schools and boys clubs for the poor. He was also the founder of the Salesian congregation. St John Bosco taught that God loves and treasures every individual.

GUZMAN: ls named after St Dominic Guzman (I170-1221), the Preacher. He lived in Spain and was the founder of the Dominican Sisters. St Dominic Guzman encouraged his followers to be compassionate and to seek knowledge and truth.

SAVIO: ls named after St Dominic Savio who was born in 1842 and died at the early age of 15 years, the model for youth.
